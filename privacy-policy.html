Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ABC Plumbing ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our services and automated SMS communications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Phone numbers — when you call us, submit a contact form, or request a quote.
  • Name and contact details — provided voluntarily through our website or intake process.
  • Service details — information about the job or service you're requesting.
  • Call metadata — timestamps and delivery status for SMS communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to missed calls with an automated SMS notification.
  • Follow up on quotes, schedule appointments, and confirm service details.
  • Request reviews after a completed job.
  • Improve our service and communication reliability.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. SMS Messaging

If you call ABC Plumbing and we are unable to answer, you may receive an automated text message in response to your call. This message is sent as a direct reply to your inbound contact.

  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP at any time to opt out of future messages.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us.

4. Data Retention

We retain contact and message records for up to 12 months for operational purposes. Records are deleted after this period unless retention is required by law.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services including Telnyx (SMS delivery) and Airtable (data management) to operate our communications platform. Each provider maintains their own data security and privacy practices.

7. Your Rights

  • Opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP.
  • Request removal of your contact information by emailing us directly.
